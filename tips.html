Eco-Friendly Tips 🌿

Small actions can make a big difference. Follow these eco-friendly tips to live a sustainable life.

♻️ Reduce Plastic

Use reusable bags and avoid single-use plastic items.

💧 Save Water

Turn off taps when not in use and fix leaks.

🔌 Save Electricity

Switch off lights and use energy-efficient appliances.

🌳 Plant Trees

Planting trees helps improve air quality and supports nature.

🚲 Use Public Transport

Reduce pollution by using buses, trains, or cycling.

🛍️ Buy Eco Products

Choose sustainable and biodegradable products.
